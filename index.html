FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to be a CPA to use this? +
Not at all. RepsRecord is designed for investors to use themselves, ideally alongside their tax professional. The built-in LTR and STR rules pages explain every IRC requirement in plain English. Your CPA will love the audit report it generates.
What's the difference between REPS and the STR loophole? +
REPS (IRC §469(c)(7)) requires 750 hours and >50% of your personal services in real property — it makes ALL your rental losses non-passive. The STR loophole requires only that your average rental period is ≤7 days and you materially participate — it makes that specific STR's losses non-passive without needing REPS. You may qualify for one, both, or neither. RepsRecord tracks them independently.
Can my spouse log hours too? +
Yes — enable spouse tracking in Settings. Spouse hours are correctly applied per the IRC: they count toward STR Test 1 (500 hours) but are tracked as a separate "other participant" for Tests 3 and 7, where your hours must exceed any individual's — including your spouse's.
Is this a substitute for a tax professional? +
No — RepsRecord is a documentation and tracking tool, not tax advice. We strongly recommend working with a tax professional who specializes in real estate. This tool helps you give them the documentation they need to defend your position.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
You can export your complete time log at any time via the Audit Report page before canceling. We recommend printing or saving your audit report before your subscription ends.
